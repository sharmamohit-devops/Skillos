# 🎨 Layout Improvements Summary

## ✅ **COMPLETED CHANGES**

### **1. Results Page - Removed Buttons & Tabs**
- ❌ **Removed**: "Download PDF Report" and "New Analysis" buttons from navbar
- ❌ **Removed**: Export section at bottom of page

- ❌ **Removed**: Advanced Analysis tabs (Overview/Advanced Analysis)

- ✅ **Updated**: 3 cards now arranged vertically instead of side-by-side:
  1. **Recommended Tools** (with wrench icon)
  2. **Focus Areas** (with layers icon)

- ✅ **Better Visual**: More engaging and realistic road appearance

### **4. Navbar Simplification**
- ❌ **Removed**: "New Analysis" button from enhanced navbar
- ✅ **Cleaner**: Simplified right section with only essential elements
- ✅ **Focused**: Better emphasis on navigation breadcrumbs
